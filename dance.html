Dance / Ambient

I try to illustrate the night life scene. I expose the euphoric sensation when we are on a joyful celebrating mood that motivates us to start dancing. In terms of music, the chord progressions that generally appear in this type of music sometimes can be addictive to the brain. Thus, the subconscious does not want the melody to stop because it is pleasant to the ear.
