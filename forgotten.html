Rescue of forgotten music

There have been many composers whose works never transcended and most of the times those works could have a value in musical quality, but the composers die, and nobody remembers their songs. These melodies can be: overtures, folk songs, marches, waltzes or pasodoble. In my presentations, I always try to include at least two songs of this type.
