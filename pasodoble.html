Pasodoble

For me, the metal instruments combined with wood instruments, create a particular sound that is so characteristic in pasodoble. When the sound waves of pasodoble emerge, they can grab the attention of any type of audience because of the imposing, sober and sensual movements that predominate in this genre. Furthermore, it is thrilling to listen to the changing of mode that can be found in most of the pasodobles going from mystery to joy and hapiness.

  • Pasodoble Cinco Villas: An organization that makes events to raise funds for a mentally ill children's home: “House of la Divina Providencia”. cincovillas.org


  • España Cañi: A pasodoble written by Pascual Marquina Narro a Spanish composer born in 1873, this song was successfully released in New York by the argentine flamenco dancer and singer: “La Argentinita”. The melody or main musical motive of this piece is recognized around the world. The word “Cañi” is a Gypsy word. I tried to make the song sound more fluid and imposing, I added some extra notes on the counterpoint to the original arrangement, everything without changing the author essence.
