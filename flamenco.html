Flamenco

One of the elements that I would highlight of this music genre is the rare but magical sounds that can be produced with only one guitar or piano. Sometimes combined with voice, tapping and clapping. Always remarking that flamenco for years has been a way of life for the millenary group called the gypsyes or romani. Flamenco has strong roots of big cultures of places like India, Africa and the Middle East.
